Beads

People have been making and trading beads for about as long as people have been making and trading anything. The beads I make are a fillacello pattern colored with gold and silver fumes and each bead has a hidden black light pattern. They can be worn solo or strung together into a long chain. Beads are available in my store, each bead is individually made and has subtle variations in color. Beads are sold individually but if you are interested in multiple beads discounts are available.
